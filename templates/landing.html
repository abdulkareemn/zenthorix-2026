Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about ProctorAI? Find quick answers to common queries about our system.

How does AI proctoring work?

ProctorAI uses continuous webcam streams and audio feeds to verify candidate identity, check gaze direction, detect secondary hardware resources or vocal helpers, and track active browser actions.

Can candidates take exams remotely?

Yes. Candidates can complete exams from any remote location using a simple computer setup equipped with a functioning webcam, microphone, and stable internet connection.

How is cheating detected?

Our background AI models detect anomalies like facial absence, screen-sharing triggers, tab-switching behaviors, unauthorized program compiles, and vocal spikes. These anomalies are instantly logged as warnings.

Is student data secure?

Yes. All exam logs, identity uploads, and results records are encrypted both in transit and at rest. Student profiles and records comply strictly with industry standards and GDPR directives.

Can the platform scale for large exams?

Absolutely. ProctorAI runs on scalable cloud infrastructure capable of hosting and monitoring thousands of concurrent candidates simultaneously without latencies.
